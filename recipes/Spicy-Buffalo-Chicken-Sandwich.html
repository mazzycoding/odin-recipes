Spicy Buffalo Chicken Sandwich

Description

This spicy Buffalo chicken sandwich recipe gives you a healthier take on the game-day fave so you can get your wing fix â€” without getting your hands dirty.

Ingredients

  • 4 (5 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup bottled Buffalo wing sauce
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 Ball Park Golden Hamburger Buns
  • 4 leaves Bibb lettuce
  • 4 slices beefsteak tomato
  • 1/4 cup blue cheese salad dressing

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Brush chicken breasts with wing sauce; season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Cook chicken breasts on the preheated grill, basting with wing sauce, until no longer pink in the center, the juices run clear, and fully cooked. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  4. Drizzle each hamburger bun with 1 tablespoon olive oil; grill until golden brown.
  5. Arrange 1 lettuce leaf on each bottom bun; top each with 1 tomato slice, 1 chicken breast, 1 tablespoon blue cheese dressing, and top bun.
